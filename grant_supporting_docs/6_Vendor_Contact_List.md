## HOW TO REQUEST QUOTATIONS

### Step 1: Prepare Your Request
- Use the RFQ templates provided in this documentation
- Customize with your specific requirements
- Include project timeline and budget range

### Step 2: Contact Vendors
- Email is the preferred method for initial contact
- Reference "Pryro Ltd. - Grant Application" in subject line
- Attach relevant RFQ documents

### Step 3: Follow Up
- Allow 5-7 business days for response
- Follow up if no response within 10 days
- Schedule calls for complex quotations

### Step 4: Document Responses
- Save all quotations in PDF format
- Create comparison spreadsheet
- Note any special terms or conditions

---

## TIPS FOR GETTING BETTER QUOTATIONS

1. **Be Specific:** Provide detailed requirements and volumes
2. **Mention Scale:** Highlight your growth plans (98 to 2,000 customers)
3. **Ask for Discounts:** Inquire about startup programs, annual prepayment discounts
4. **Request Itemization:** Ask for detailed breakdowns, not just total costs
5. **Negotiate:** Most vendors have flexibility, especially for annual commitments
6. **Bundle Services:** Ask about package deals for multiple services
7. **Reference Competition:** Mention you're comparing multiple vendors
8. **Timeline:** Indicate you need quotation for grant application (adds urgency)
