## How to Use These Documents

### For Grant Application Submission

1. **Include Master Cost Breakdown** (`1_Cost_Breakdown_Master_Spreadsheet.md`)
   - This is your primary supporting document
   - Shows detailed itemization of all costs
   - Demonstrates grant funding vs. co-investment split

2. **Attach Sample Quotations** (Files 2, 3, 7)
   - Provides evidence of estimated costs
   - Shows you've researched market rates
   - Demonstrates due diligence

3. **Reference Vendor List** (File 6)
   - Shows you've identified multiple vendors
   - Demonstrates competitive pricing research
   - Provides transparency in cost estimation

### For Obtaining Official Quotations

1. **Use RFQ Templates** (Files 4, 5)
   - Customize with your specific details
   - Send to vendors from contact list (File 6)
   - Request responses within 2-3 weeks

2. **Follow Up Process**
   - Send RFQs to 3-5 vendors per category
   - Allow 5-7 business days for response
   - Follow up if no response within 10 days
   - Compare quotations and select best value

3. **Document Official Quotations**
   - Save all responses in PDF format
   - Create comparison spreadsheet
   - Attach to grant application as additional supporting docs

## Important Notes

### About Sample Quotations

The sample quotations (Files 2, 3, 7) are **realistic estimates** based on current market rates but are **not official vendor quotations**. They are provided to:
- Demonstrate cost estimation methodology
- Show format and detail level expected
- Support grant application if official quotes are delayed

**Recommendation:** Obtain official quotations from actual vendors using the RFQ templates provided.

### About Cost Estimates

All cost estimates are based on:
- Current market rates (April 2026)
- Industry standard pricing
- Similar project benchmarks
- Vendor published pricing (where available)

Actual costs may vary by ±10-15% depending on:
- Negotiated discounts
- Volume commitments
- Payment terms (annual vs. monthly)
- Specific requirements

### About Exchange Rates

- Exchange rate used: 1 USD = 1,350 FRW
- Rate as of: April 2026
- Verify current rate before final submission
- Consider including exchange rate disclaimer in application
