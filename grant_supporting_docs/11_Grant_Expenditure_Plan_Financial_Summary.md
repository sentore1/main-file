## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This expenditure report outlines how Pryro Ltd. will utilize the requested $100,000 grant funding over a 12-month period to scale from 98 to 2,000 customers across Rwanda, United States, and Arab markets. The grant will be allocated across six strategic activity areas with clear milestones, deliverables, and financial accountability.

### Key Allocation:
- **30%** - Sales Team Expansion (people)
- **20%** - AI Marketing Automation (tools)
- **25%** - Market Localization (expansion)
- **10%** - AI Client Features (product)
- **10%** - Infrastructure & Security (foundation)
- **5%** - Operations & Contingency (management)
