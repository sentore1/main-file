## MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY

### Business Performance

Pryro Ltd. successfully launched operations in Q4 2024, acquiring 12 paying customers and generating $19,400 in revenue during the initial 3-month period. The company achieved profitability in its first quarter of operations with a net income of $3,350 (17.3% net margin).

### Key Achievements

1. **Product Launch:** Successfully launched Rwanda's first locally-developed ERP platform
2. **Customer Acquisition:** Acquired 12 customers across diverse sectors (distribution, retail, manufacturing)
3. **Profitability:** Achieved positive cash flow and profitability from month 1
4. **Team Building:** Assembled a 10-person team with strong technical and business capabilities

### Challenges

1. **Limited Marketing Budget:** Customer acquisition primarily through direct sales and referrals
2. **Infrastructure Costs:** Cloud hosting costs higher than projected due to performance requirements
3. **Market Education:** Significant time required to educate customers on ERP benefits

### Outlook for 2025

With proven product-market fit and positive unit economics, Pryro is positioned for significant growth in 2025. The company projects:
- 86 additional customers (total 98 by end of 2025)
- $48,600 in additional revenue
- Continued profitability with reinvestment in sales and product development
