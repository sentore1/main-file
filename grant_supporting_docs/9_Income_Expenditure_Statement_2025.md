## MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY

### Business Performance

2025 was a foundational year for Pryro Ltd., focused on building the team, refining the product, and establishing market presence. While the company operated at a loss of $87,470, this was expected and planned for as part of the startup investment phase.

### Key Achievements

1. **Customer Growth:** Acquired 86 new customers, growing from 12 to 98 customers (717% growth)
2. **Revenue Growth:** Generated $38,000 in revenue, 96% increase from 2024 run-rate
3. **Team Building:** Assembled a complete 10-person team across all functions
4. **Product Development:** Enhanced platform with new features based on customer feedback
5. **Market Validation:** Achieved 85% customer retention, proving product-market fit

### Financial Highlights

- **Gross Margin:** 78.8% demonstrates strong unit economics
- **Revenue Growth:** 96% quarter-over-quarter growth from Q1 to Q4
- **Customer Acquisition:** Successfully acquired customers at $1,017 CAC
- **Funding:** Secured $70,000 in founder investment and financing

### Challenges

1. **High Operating Costs:** Personnel costs of $95,220 significantly exceeded revenue
2. **Slow Customer Acquisition:** Limited marketing budget constrained growth
3. **Cash Flow:** Required external financing to maintain operations
4. **Market Education:** Significant effort required to educate market on ERP benefits

### Path to Profitability

With 98 customers and $4,500 monthly recurring revenue by year-end, Pryro is positioned for profitability in 2026-2027 through:

1. **Revenue Scale:** Growing to 500-2,000 customers
2. **Operating Leverage:** Fixed costs spread across larger customer base
3. **Improved Unit Economics:** Reduced CAC through marketing automation
4. **Expanded Revenue Streams:** Additional services and premium features

### Outlook for 2026

With grant funding and continued growth, Pryro projects:
- 500+ customers by end of 2026
- $250,000+ in annual revenue
- Break-even operations by Q4 2026
- Positive cash flow by Q1 2027
